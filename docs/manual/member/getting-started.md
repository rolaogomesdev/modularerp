# Getting started

Welcome! Modular ERP is where your company manages day-to-day work — your absences, payslips, expenses, tasks on the shop floor. It works best on your phone.

## 1. Create your account

You'll receive an **invitation email** from your company.

1. Tap the invitation link.
2. Choose a password (or sign in if you already have an account from another company).
3. Set up **two-step verification** — this is required, see below.

## 2. Two-step verification (required)

Your account protects personal data like payslips, so a password alone is not enough. At first sign-in you'll set up a **verification app** (Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, 1Password…):

1. Open your authenticator app and scan the QR code shown.
2. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm.

From then on, signing in = password + the 6-digit code from your app. If you ever lose your phone, contact your administrator or support — after confirming your identity they can reset two-step verification. Full details: [Signing in & two-step verification](../concepts/sign-in-and-2fa.md).

## 3. Install the app on your phone

Modular ERP is a web app you can install — no app store needed:

- **Android/Chrome**: menu ⋮ → *Add to Home screen*.
- **iPhone/Safari**: Share □↑ → *Add to Home Screen*.

## 4. Find your way around

The bottom of the screen has four tabs:

- **Home** — your day: pending requests, notifications, your team.
- **Modules** — everything your company enabled (HR, Finance…). You only see what you have access to.
- **Approvals** — things waiting for you (if any).
- **Search** — find anything, or **ask the assistant** a question like "how do I request vacation?" — it answers and takes you to the right screen.

Your **profile** lives behind your avatar, top right: photo, language (Português/English), light or dark theme, and security settings. See [Personal profile & security](../concepts/personal-profile-and-security.md).

## 5. What you can see and do

Your access is set by your company through **roles and teams** — if a button or module is missing, you don't have that permission yet. The app (and the assistant) will always tell you *which* permission you'd need, so you can ask your administrator. More in [Companies, teams & roles](../concepts/companies-teams-roles.md).

## If you work in more than one company

One account works everywhere. Switch companies from the company name at the top left. Each company sees only its own data about you — your personal profile stays yours.
