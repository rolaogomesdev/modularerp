# Getting started as a team manager

You manage a team: you approve requests, keep an eye on who's in and out, and (depending on your role) run shop-floor work. Everything a [member](../member/getting-started.md) can do applies to you too — start there for account setup, two-step verification and installing the app.

## Your extra powers

Managers hold a role like **Supervisor** *within a team*. That means your extra permissions usually apply **only to your team** — you'll see your team's absences, not the whole company's. If you manage two teams, you'll have the role in both.

## The Approvals tab is your inbox

Everything waiting for your decision lands in **Approvals** (bottom tab, with a badge count): absence requests, expenses over a threshold, and so on.

1. Tap an item to see the details and history.
2. **Approve** or **Reject** — a rejection asks for a short reason, which the requester sees.
3. Done items move to the history view. Every decision is recorded.

You cannot approve your own requests — someone else with the permission always has to.

## Your team, day to day

- **Home** shows your team today: who's absent, what needs attention.
- Each module gives you team views — e.g. **HR → Absences** shows the team calendar and balances.
- Ask the assistant things like *"who is out next week in my team?"* — it answers only with what your permissions allow.

## Going on vacation? Delegate

You can hand your approvals to a colleague for a fixed period:

> **Where**: Settings → Delegations *(or ask your company admin)*
> **Needs**: your manager role; the delegation is time-bound and recorded

Pick the person, the role to delegate, and start/end dates. It expires automatically — nothing to remember to undo.
