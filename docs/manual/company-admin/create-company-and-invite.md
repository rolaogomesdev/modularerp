# Create your company & invite people

## Create the company

**Navigation: home screen (`/`) → *Create a company***

1. Sign in (see [Signing in & two-step verification](../concepts/sign-in-and-2fa.md)).
2. On your home screen, fill in **Company name** — the **web address** is suggested automatically (e.g. *Padaria Silva* → `/c/padaria-silva`). You can adjust it: lowercase letters, numbers and hyphens only. It cannot be changed casually later, so pick something short.
3. Press **Create company**. You land on your new company's page as its first active member.

> Creating a company makes you its founder. Role templates (Owner, HR Manager…) arrive with the permissions phase; until then, every active member has the same abilities.

## Invite a colleague

**Navigation: `/c/[company]` → *Invite someone***

1. Enter the colleague's **email address** — the invitation only works for exactly this address.
2. Press **Create invitation** and **copy the link** that appears.
3. Send them the link however you like (email sending from the app arrives later).

The person appears in the members list as **Invited** until they accept.

## What the colleague does

1. Open the invitation link.
2. Sign in — or create an account first if they don't have one (with the **invited email**), including the required two-step verification.
3. Press **Accept invitation**. The company appears on their home screen and they show as **Active** in your members list.

## Rules worth knowing

- One pending invitation per email per company; creating a duplicate is rejected.
- An invitation link is **single-use** and stops working once accepted.
- Only the invited email can accept — signing in with a different account shows a permission error.
- People can belong to several companies with one account; the switcher (company name, top left) moves between them.
