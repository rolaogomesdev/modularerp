# Your personal profile & account security

Your **profile is yours**, not your company's. It travels with your account across every company you belong to.

## What lives in your profile

> **Where**: tap your avatar (top right) → **Profile**

- **Photo and display name** — how colleagues see you.
- **Language** — Português or English, effective immediately.
- **Theme** — Light, Dark, or *System* (follows your phone). Dark is easier on the eyes in low light — popular on the shop floor.
- **Notifications** — what reaches you and how (in-app, email).

## What your company manages instead

Contract, salary, work address and similar **employment records** belong to the company's HR module and are managed by people with HR permissions — you'll always find *your own* copy under HR → My data. If you leave a company, your account and profile remain yours; only that company's employment records close.

## Security

> **Where**: avatar → **Security**

- **Password** — change it here; you'll confirm with your current one.
- **Two-step verification** — required for everyone, always. Manage your authenticator apps here, and keep your **recovery codes** safe: they are the only way back in if you lose your phone. Lost both? Contact your administrator — identity is re-verified before any reset; nobody will ever ask you for your codes.
- **Active sessions** — every device signed in as you, with time and place. See something strange? **Sign it out** immediately and change your password.

## Privacy, briefly

- Colleagues see only your name and photo — never your preferences, sessions or security settings.
- Each company sees only its own records about you; companies cannot see each other.
- You can ask for a copy of your data or its removal — your administrator starts the (GDPR) process, and removal respects legally required records like payroll history.
