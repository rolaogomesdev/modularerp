# Your personal profile & account security

Your **profile is yours**, not your company's. It travels with your account across every company you belong to.

## What lives in your profile

> **Where**: the person icon in the top bar (or *Your profile* at the bottom of the sidebar) → **Your profile**

- **Photo and name** — how colleagues see you. Photos up to 2 MB (PNG/JPEG/WebP).
- **Language** — Português or English, effective immediately.
- **Theme** — Light, Dark, or *Follow the device*. Dark is easier on the eyes in low light — popular on the shop floor. Applies on every device you sign into.
- **Notifications** — your delivery choices are saved now; delivery itself starts when notifications ship.

## What your company manages instead

Contract, salary, work address and similar **employment records** belong to the company's HR module and are managed by people with HR permissions — you'll always find *your own* copy under HR → My data once HR ships. If you leave a company, your account and profile remain yours; only that company's employment records close.

## Security

> **Where**: Your profile → **Security**

- **Password** — set a new one here (at least 8 characters).
- **Two-step verification devices** — required for everyone, always. You can add a second authenticator (a tablet, a password manager) or remove one. Removing your **last** device takes you straight back to setup — that's the intended way to move to a new phone. Lost the phone entirely? Contact your administrator — identity is re-verified before any reset.
- **Sessions** — one tap signs out every other browser and phone while keeping the one in your hand. If anything ever looks strange, do that and change your password.

## Privacy, briefly

- Colleagues see only your name and photo — never your preferences, devices or security settings.
- Each company sees only its own records about you; companies cannot see each other.
- You can ask for a copy of your data or its removal — your administrator starts the (GDPR) process, and removal respects legally required records like payroll history.
